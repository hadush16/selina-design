About Selina Design

Selina Design (ሰሊና ዲዛይን) celebrates Ethiopian heritage with modern tailoring. Based in Addis Ababa, our goal is to preserve traditional techniques while offering contemporary styles.

Our Mission

We craft high-quality Habesha dresses and jewelry using authentic materials and careful handwork. Each piece is designed to feel personal and timeless.
